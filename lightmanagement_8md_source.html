4 - Lights are components associated with an entity.
5 - Lights can be attached to any of the entities of a given scene.
6 - Lights are loaded by a LightManager system. If no system is able to load lights, there will be no lights in the scene
7 except to one that can be attached to the camera.
8 - The way lights are used in the engine is specific to the renderer.
9 - A renderer must, in its initializeInternal method, create and register a lightManager system.
10 - The system will receive fileData at loading and will be polled by the renderer to get light to render.
